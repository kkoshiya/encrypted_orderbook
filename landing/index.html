How It Works

1

Encrypt Your Order

Your order details (price, quantity) are encrypted using Fully Homomorphic Encryption before leaving your device.

2

Submit to Orderbook

The encrypted order is submitted to the orderbook and stored alongside other encrypted orders.

3

Homomorphic Matching

The orderbook performs matching operations directly on the encrypted data without ever decrypting it.

4

Secure Settlement

When a match is found, the trade is settled on Solana while preserving the privacy of the original orders.

ElizaOS Integration

Interact with Natural Language

The FHE Orderbook is integrated as an action in ElizaOS, allowing users to interact with the orderbook using natural language through any ElizaOS agent.

Place Orders

Simply tell an ElizaOS agent to place a buy or sell order with your desired price and quantity.

View Orderbook

Ask to see the current state of the orderbook to make informed trading decisions.
